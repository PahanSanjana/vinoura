## Usage Tips
1. **Primary**: Matte Black + Off-White (main content)
2. **Accent**: Champagne Gold (CTAs, highlights)
3. **Secondary**: Soft Charcoal (backgrounds, subtle elements)
4. **Tertiary**: Emerald Green, Midnight Navy (special accents)
5. **Neutral**: Platinum (borders, dividers)
